What We Do

Our Services

From painting to plumbing, we handle the work your home needs. Every project gets the same attention to detail.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, doors, cabinets — crisp lines and even coverage that transforms your space. We prep right so the finish lasts.

Exterior Painting

Fences, pool areas, garage floors, house exteriors. We use weather-rated coatings built for South Florida sun and storms.

Drywall Install & Repair

New construction, patches, water damage — we install, tape, mud, and sand to a smooth finish ready for paint.

Texture & Popcorn Removal

Dated popcorn ceilings gone. Smooth, knockdown, or orange peel texture applied to walls and ceilings. Clean, modern look.

Wood Staining & Waterproofing

Decks, fences, pergolas, docks — stained and sealed against Florida humidity. Protection that looks good and lasts.

Baseboard & Crown Molding

Precise cuts, tight joints, caulked and painted. We install baseboards, crown molding, and trim that elevate any room.

Door Installation

Interior and exterior doors — hung, trimmed, and finished. Prehung or slab, we handle the full install.

Laminate & Wood Flooring

Click-lock laminate, engineered hardwood, or solid planks. Level subfloor, clean transitions, and a finished look you can walk on same day.

Plumbing Repairs

Water leaks, faucet replacements, shower installs, toilet repairs. We fix what drips, leaks, or won't stop running.
